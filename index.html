Recipe #1

    Pancakes

Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2
flour 150g
salt 50g
milk 300ml

Preparation:

Put flour, eggs, salt and milk in a bowl and mix thoroughly with a foam to make a smooth mixture. On a heated pan of low-fat pancakes, pour in the casting paste with a pen, and spread it over the entire surface of the pan with speed. When the pancake on the surface changes color, turn the pallets over and briefly bake on other pages. Fill pancakes with salt or sweet stuffing if desired.

Recipe #2

    Breath
Ingredients Quantity
water 3dl
egg 1
salt 19g
yeast 20g

Preparation:

Mix all the ingredients together with the mixer for a few minutes until a smooth dough is obtained. Let the dough stand for half an hour Turn on the oven at 250 degrees and place a refractory bowl in which the bread will be baked to heat The bread no longer needs to be kneaded, just shaped according to the bowl. Wrap it in a kitchen cloth to rest and cool slightly.
